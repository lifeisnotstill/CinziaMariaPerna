## BIO (English)

My name is Cinzia Maria Perna, and music has always been a part of my life. After many years spent living and working between Italy and abroad, building a beautiful family, and experiencing different cultures, I have finally returned to my greatest passion—singing.

From a young age, I was always singing and dancing. During my high school and university years, music became a big part of my world—I performed with several bands and often took the stage in Campania and across southern Italy. But life took me in a different direction. Work, responsibilities, and the need to build a future led me away from music. When I moved to the United States, I had to start from scratch, and for many years, singing became a distant memory.

Then, during the Covid years, something reignited inside me. I felt the urge to sing again, so I began taking online vocal lessons. Through this journey, I met some musician friends in California who helped me reconnect with music in a way I hadn't felt in years. In a short time, I realized not only that my voice was still strong and expressive, but also that my years of traveling—through Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and the United States—had profoundly shaped my musical taste and artistic identity.

Today, I approach music with a renewed sense of purpose. My repertoire blends soul, R&B, and traditional pop, creating intimate and emotional moments alongside uplifting, groovy rhythms that get people moving. Through singing, I tell stories of journeys, encounters, and emotions, transforming each performance into a heartfelt and immersive experience.

## BIO (Italiano)

Mi chiamo Cinzia Maria Perna e la musica è sempre stata parte della mia vita. Dopo tanti anni trascorsi tra l'Italia e l'estero, costruendo una bellissima famiglia e vivendo esperienze che mi hanno arricchita profondamente, sono finalmente tornata al canto, la mia più grande passione.

Fin da giovanissima, ho sempre cantato e ballato. Durante gli anni del liceo e dell'università, la musica era il mio mondo: facevo parte di diverse band e mi esibivo spesso in Campania e nel sud Italia. Poi la vita mi ha portata altrove. Il lavoro, gli impegni e la necessità di costruirmi un futuro mi hanno allontanata dalla musica. Quando mi sono trasferita negli Stati Uniti, ho dovuto ricominciare tutto da capo, e per molti anni il canto è rimasto in un angolo della mia vita.

Durante il periodo del Covid, però, ho sentito il bisogno di riprendere a cantare. Ho iniziato a prendere lezioni di canto online e, grazie all'incontro con alcuni amici musicisti in California, ho riscoperto quella parte di me che pensavo di aver lasciato indietro. Nel giro di poco tempo, ho capito non solo di avere ancora una bella voce e tante cose da esprimere attraverso il canto, ma anche che tutti i miei viaggi e le esperienze vissute in Africa, Europa, Medio Oriente e Stati Uniti avevano arricchito il mio rapporto con la musica in modi che non avevo mai immaginato.

Oggi interpreto la musica con una nuova consapevolezza. Il mio repertorio spazia dal soul all'R&B, fino all'easy listening, con brani che creano atmosfere intime ed emozionanti, ma anche momenti di puro groove e divertimento. Attraverso il canto, racconto storie di viaggi, di incontri e di emozioni vissute, trasformando ogni esibizione in un'esperienza autentica e coinvolgente.

### Album Description

*Old, New, Boundless, & Blue* was born from an unexpected meeting. Introduced by a mutual friend, Cinzia and Stefano connected instantly over a shared love of music. What began as casual conversations evolved into a creative synergy—songs and ideas that naturally came together to form something deeply personal.

Around this shared passion, extraordinary musicians—among the finest on the Italian music scene—came together to bring a unique project to life. The title embodies everything they are and everything they wanted to infuse into the music: a celebration of the freedom to embrace different eras, styles, and traditions without boundaries.

This album spans from international pop to classic Neapolitan melodies, enriched by Mediterranean atmospheres and Middle Eastern influences. From Iyeoka's powerful storytelling to Pink Martini's vibrant mix of languages and cultures, and the timeless melodies of Naples, each song represents a piece of their lives and the emotions that guided them.

This is a labor of love, crafted by hand, heart, and soul. Real musicians, recorded in real time, in the inspiring setting of a studio. No shortcuts, no tricks—just the raw, beautiful magic of human creativity.

*— Cinzia & Stefano*

## DESIGN NOTES

### Overall Vibe
Jazz club meets Mediterranean coast. Warm, intimate, sophisticated. Classy, not flashy.

### Color Palette — Primary (Perna & Nerozzi)
Drawn from the album artwork:
- **Background:** Near-black `#0d0d0d` / deep charcoal `#1a1a1a`
- **Primary accent:** Warm pink/magenta `#e0337a` (from album cover piano keys motif)
- **Secondary accent:** Warm gold/cream `#f5f0e8`
- **Text:** Off-white `#f0ece4`
- **Muted text:** Light grey `#9a9a9a`

### Color Palette — Black Honey section
The Black Honey logo is dark with gold — lean into this for its section:
- **Background:** Near-black `#0a0a0a`
- **Accent:** Gold `#c9a84c`
- **Text:** Cream `#f5f0e8`

### Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif — matches the script style of the album title)
- **Body:** Inter or Lato (clean, readable)
- **Accent / quotes:** Consider a light script font for pull quotes (like the "Cinzia" signature on the CD)

### Visual Motifs from Album Artwork
The CD digifile is a useful reference. Key design elements:
- **Piano keys strip** (black/white/pink vertical bars) — used as a decorative divider on the CD; could work as a section separator on the site
- **Script title treatment** — the hand-lettered feel of "Old, New, Boundless, & Blue" on the cover
- **B&W photography** with full-color accents (the studio photos on the CD are B&W)
- **Grid of musician portraits** — consider a similar approach for the credits section

English / Italiano — site should support both. Bio and Black Honey description are available in both languages above.
